Tatiana Bischak is an award winning (in Norway) designer and illustrator living in Greystones, Ireland.

She has worked with Adobe, Zappos, 8x8, Dr Pepper, and many startups to develop branding and illustrative products.

Her approach to work is very much based in brand identity and the why and will only take on goal focused projects. She is an advocate for ethical treatment of workers- particularly artists- mental health, and the betterment of culture and society starting with the self.
